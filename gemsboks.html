GEMSBOK

The gemsbok, gemsbuck or South African oryx is a large antelope in the genus Oryx. It is native to the arid regions of Southern Africa, such as the Kalahari Desert. Some authorities formerly included the East African as a subspecies.

Physical: The gemsbok is a large antelope of striking appearance with long spear-like horns. The only outward difference between males and females is their horns. For this reason, many hunters mistake females for males each year.
